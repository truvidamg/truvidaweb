Safety notes

Common + normal
  • Brief injection-site soreness (24 hours)
  • Bright yellow urine after B-complex or B12
  • Warmth or flushing (vitamin C, niacin)
  • Energy lift within hours (B-vitamins)
  • Improved mood or sleep over 1–2 weeks (D3)
Requires provider oversight
  • Vitamin D — toxicity possible; lab-guided dosing only
  • Iron — serious reactions possible; always in-office administration
  • High-dose C — contraindicated in G6PD, kidney stones, kidney disease
  • Any allergic reaction — seek care immediately
